Sometimes you just want a favicon without the fuss.
Favicons are getting close to being deprecated. This tool is for those who want to stifle favicon 404 errors on the backend and frontend.

You should always include the blank Favicon <link> directive on every page, aswell as a real favicon in the root of your domain. You needn't put much effort into creating teh perfect favicon.
